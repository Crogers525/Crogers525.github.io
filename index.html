About Me

Hello, I'm Casey Rogers

With over a decade of experience, I specialize in creating engaging, standard-aligned learning experiences for adult and professional learners, particularly in technical and workforce-focused domains.

Grounded in education and instructional technology, I bring a learner-centered, hands-on approach to design that emphasizes real-world application, problem-solving, and skill transfer.

My experience developing high-impact training for complex technical subjects — mainly Uncrewed Aircraft Systems (UAS) — has strengthened my ability to translate highly sophisticated concepts into clear, accessible, and engaging instruction.

An early foundation in K–12 education gives me a deep understanding of how learners build knowledge across age groups and contexts.

Current Role
Instructional Designer at Mississippi State University (CITE), partnering with faculty to design and review online/hybrid courses using best practices and Quality Matters standards.
My Approach
I apply Instructional Systems Design (ISD) principles to create engaging, accessible, and results-driven learning experiences grounded in theory and informed by data.
Background
Former classroom teacher, curriculum coordinator, and instructional innovator with experience spanning K–12, higher education, and technical workforce training.

Program Reflection

MSIT Reflection

Motivated by the need to improve training courses for first responders learning drone operations, I enrolled in the MSIT program to develop stronger instructional design skills.

Throughout the program, I gained knowledge of key theories and strategies such as instructional graphics, cognitive load theory, dual-channel processing, and Gagne's Nine Events of Instruction.

The program also helped me build critical skills in needs analysis, aligning instruction with organizational goals, and using technology intentionally to support learning.

Hands-on projects and real-world applications prepared me to confidently address instructional challenges in my career. Overall, the MSIT program provided a strong foundation in both theory and practice.
